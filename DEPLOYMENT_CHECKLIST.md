requirements.txt --force-reinstall\n```\n\n### Issue: Port 5000 already in use\n**Solution:**\n```powershell\n# Find process on port 5000\nnetstat -ano | findstr :5000\n\n# Kill the process (replace PID)\ntaskkill /PID 1234 /F\n\n# Or change port in app.py\n# Change: app.run(port=5000)\n# To: app.run(port=5001)\n```\n\n### Issue: Angel One shows \"Disconnected\"\n**Solution:**\n```powershell\n#
